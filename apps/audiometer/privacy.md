## 1. Introduction
Audiometer ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Audiometer mobile application ("App"), including all related features, tools, and services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Microphone Data
The App requires access to your device's microphone to measure ambient sound levels in decibels (dB). The App processes this audio data locally on your device to calculate noise measurements:
- **Local Processing Only:** Audio data is processed in real-time on your device to calculate decibel levels
- **No Recording:** The App does not record, store, or transmit actual audio files
- **No Transmission:** Raw microphone data is never sent to our servers or third parties
- **Calculated Metrics Only:** Only calculated decibel measurements and noise levels may be stored locally

### 2.2 Device Information
We may collect limited technical information including:
- Device model and operating system version
- App version and installation date
- General usage statistics (feature usage, session duration)

### 2.3 Advertising Information
The App uses Google Mobile Ads SDK to display advertisements. Google may collect:
- Advertising ID from your device
- General location information (country/region level)
- App usage patterns and interaction data

For details on Google's data practices, please see [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### 2.4 In-App Purchase Information
When you make purchases (such as removing ads), transaction information is processed through Google Play Services:
- Purchase history and preferences
- Transaction amounts (not credit card details - handled by Google Play)

Credit card and payment information is handled exclusively by Google Play and is subject to their privacy policies.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
- To provide and improve the App's core functionality (noise measurement and visualization)
- To display personalized advertisements through Google Mobile Ads
- To process in-app purchases for ad removal
- To analyze app usage patterns and improve user experience
- To maintain app security and prevent fraud
- To comply with legal obligations

## 4. Data Storage and Security
- **Local Storage:** Measurement history and user preferences are stored locally on your device
- **Encryption:** Your device's operating system handles encryption of local data
- **Cloud Backup:** Some data may be backed up through your device's automatic backup service (iCloud, Google Play Services)
- **No Server Storage:** We do not maintain centralized servers to store your personal audio or measurement data

## 5. Third-Party Services
Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect data:
- **Google Mobile Ads:** For displaying advertisements and analytics
- **Google Play Services:** For in-app purchases, permissions, and app distribution
- **Firebase (Google):** For crash reporting and app analytics

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them:
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Google AdMob Privacy](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)

## 6. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We do NOT share your personal data with third parties except:
- With service providers necessary to operate the App (Google, Firebase)
- As required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
- With your explicit consent

**We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise disclose your personal information to advertisers or other third parties for marketing purposes.**

## 7. Your Privacy Rights
### 7.1 Access and Control
You have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
- Opt-out of non-essential data collection

### 7.2 Device Permissions
You can manage the App's permissions through your device settings:
- Android: Settings > Apps > Audiometer > Permissions
- iOS: Settings > Privacy > Microphone

Denying microphone access will prevent the App from functioning.

### 7.3 Advertising Preferences
You can opt-out of personalized ads through Google's Ad Settings or your device's advertising ID settings.

## 8. Data Retention
- **Local Measurement Data:** Retained on your device indefinitely until you manually delete it
- **Device Information:** Retained while the App is installed
- **Advertising Data:** Managed by Google according to their retention policies
- **Uninstall:** Deleting the App will remove all locally stored data (unless backed up to cloud)

## 9. Children's Privacy
The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information to the App should contact us immediately.

## 10. International Data Transfers
Your information may be transferred to and stored in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using the App, you consent to such transfers.

## 11. Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 12. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
